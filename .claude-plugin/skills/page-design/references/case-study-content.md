# Case Study Pages — Content Requirements

## Case Studies Listing

### Page Goal
Showcase the breadth of industries served and complexity handled. Help visitors find a relevant example from their industry.

### Required Content Elements

#### Case Study Collection
- **What:** All published case studies
- **Each case needs:** Title, industry label, one hero metric, 2-line excerpt, featured image
- **Organization:** Filterable by industry and/or service type (if >6 cases)

### SEO
- H1: "Customer Case Studies" or "Project References"
- Blog schema

---

## Individual Case Study

### Page Goal
Tell the story of a specific project. Provide evidence of capability through real outcomes. Use the **Challenge → Solution → Results** framework.

### Required Content Elements

#### Project Context
- **Industry:** Which sector (automotive, electronics, industrial, consumer, medical)
- **Application:** What the component is used for
- **Customer:** Named (with permission) or anonymized ("European automotive OEM")
- **Volume:** Annual production quantity
- **Timeline:** When the project started / how long in production

#### Challenge Section
- **What the customer needed:** Technical requirements, constraints, timeline pressure
- **Why it was difficult:** Tight tolerances, complex geometry, high volume, multi-process needs
- **What wasn't working before:** Previous supplier issues, material limitations, cost problems
- **Length:** 1-2 paragraphs, specific and technical

#### Solution Section
- **Linimatic's approach:** How the challenge was addressed
- **Technical decisions:** Alloy selection, die design choices, process innovations
- **Services used:** Which of the 10 services were involved
- **Length:** 2-3 paragraphs with specific technical detail
- **Images:** Process photos, die images, production line (Recommended)

#### Results Section
- **Quantified outcomes:** Required — at least 2-3 measurable results
- **Good metrics:**
  - First-pass yield (e.g., 99.2%)
  - Cost reduction vs. previous approach (e.g., 18% less)
  - Lead time improvement
  - Production volume achieved
  - Zero field returns / defect rate
  - Die life achieved
- **Ongoing relationship:** Duration of partnership, repeat orders

#### Technical Specifications Sidebar (Recommended)
- **What:** Quick-reference data about the project
- **Fields:** Industry, alloy, annual volume, tolerances, surface finish, services used, year started
- **Purpose:** Scannable summary for technical readers

#### Component Photography (Recommended)
- **What:** High-quality images of the actual component
- **Show:** Surface finish, dimensional precision, scale reference
- **Purpose:** Visual proof of quality

#### Related Content (Recommended)
- **Related cases:** 2 case studies from same industry or using same services
- **Related services:** Links to the specific services used in this project
- **Purpose:** Keep visitors exploring; internal linking for SEO

#### Conversion Element
- **CTA:** Project-specific language — "Have a similar project? Let's discuss."

### SEO
- Article + Product schema
- Keywords: industry + application + process specific
- Breadcrumbs: Home > Cases > [Case Title]
