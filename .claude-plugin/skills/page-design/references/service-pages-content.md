# Service Pages — Content Requirements

## Services Hub

### Page Goal
Overview all service capabilities. Demonstrate the complete value chain from prototype to finished assembly. Route visitors to the specific service they need.

### Required Content Elements

#### Full Service List
- **What:** All 10 service categories with descriptions
- **Categories:** Prototypes, Casting Foundry, Post-Processing, Surface Coating, Quality Assurance, Assembly, Tooling, Design Assistance, Logistics, Sustainability
- **Each needs:** Service name, 2-3 sentence description, representative image/icon, link to detail page

#### Process Narrative
- **What:** Communicate that all services happen under one roof
- **Key message:** Complete value chain = faster delivery, better quality control, single point of contact
- **Show the flow:** Concept → Prototype → Tooling → Casting → Processing → Coating → QA → Assembly → Delivery

#### Conversion Element
- **CTA:** "Discuss your project" or "Request a quote"

### SEO
- H1: "Zinc Die-Casting Services" (or localized equivalent)
- Service schema (collection)
- Breadcrumbs: Home > Services

## Individual Service Page

### Page Goal
Deep-dive into one service capability. Convince engineers of technical competence. Convert to quote request.

### Required Content Elements

#### Service Description
- **What:** Clear explanation of the service and what it delivers
- **Length:** 2-4 paragraphs covering capability, capacity, and customer benefit
- **Tone:** Technical authority — use specific metrics, standards, and terminology

#### Technical Specifications (Required for applicable services)
- **What:** Concrete data about capability
- **Examples by service:**
  - Die-Casting: alloy types, tolerances, part weight range, machine tonnage, annual capacity, cycle times
  - Post-Processing: CNC capabilities, deburring methods, achievable tolerances
  - Surface Coating: available finishes (plating, painting, powder coating, anodizing), coating thicknesses, standards met
  - Quality: inspection equipment (CMM, etc.), statistical methods, certifications
- **Format:** Data should be scannable — tables, lists, or specification cards

#### Process Description
- **What:** How the service works from customer engagement to delivery
- **Purpose:** Reduce uncertainty for buyers who haven't worked with die-casting before
- **Content:** Step-by-step process with key decision points and deliverables at each stage

#### Related Case Studies (Recommended)
- **What:** 2-3 case studies where this service was central
- **Purpose:** Proof that the capability is proven, not theoretical

#### Related Services (Recommended)
- **What:** 2-3 complementary services
- **Purpose:** Cross-selling + SEO internal linking
- **Example:** Die-Casting page links to Post-Processing and Surface Coating

#### FAQ (Optional)
- **What:** 3-5 common questions about this specific service
- **Purpose:** Target long-tail search queries + reduce sales inquiry burden
- **Schema:** FAQPage markup for search result rich snippets

#### Conversion Element
- **CTA:** Service-specific prompt ("Get a casting quote" not generic "Contact us")

### SEO
- H1: Service name with primary keyword
- Service schema (specific type)
- Breadcrumbs: Home > Services > [This Service]
- Internal links to related services and cases

## Service Categories — Content Notes

| Service | Key Technical Content to Include |
|---------|--------------------------------|
| **Prototypes** | Turnaround time, minimum volume, soft tooling options, design iteration process |
| **Casting Foundry** | Machine fleet (count, tonnage), alloys (Zamak 2/3/5), tolerances, shot weights, cycle times |
| **Post-Processing** | CNC machine types, deburring/trimming methods, achievable tolerances post-machining |
| **Surface Coating** | Available finishes list, salt spray test hours, coating thickness ranges, color matching |
| **Quality Assurance** | Inspection equipment, SPC capability, certifications (ISO, IATF), PPAP/APQP if applicable |
| **Assembly** | Assembly methods, sub-assembly capability, insert molding, fastening |
| **Tooling** | Die design capability, multi-cavity dies, die life expectancy, maintenance program |
| **Design Assistance** | DFM review process, CAD capabilities, wall thickness guidance, draft angle recommendations |
| **Logistics** | Warehousing, kanban/JIT delivery, packaging, shipping to European markets |
| **Sustainability** | Zinc recycling rate, energy programs, environmental certifications, lead-free initiatives |
