| Intent Level | Search Behavior | Example Queries | Target Pages |

## Keyword Implementation Rules

1. **One primary keyword per page** — avoid internal keyword competition
2. **Primary keyword in h1** — naturally worded, not forced
3. **Secondary keywords in h2s** — spread across sections
4. **Long-tail in body copy** — natural usage in paragraphs
5. **Keywords in URL slug** — e.g., `/services/zinc-die-casting`
6. **Keywords in image alt text** — descriptive, not stuffed
7. **Keywords in meta title** — within first 50 characters
8. **Keywords in meta description** — within first 100 characters
