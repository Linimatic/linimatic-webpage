# Homepage — Content Requirements

## Page Goal
Establish Linimatic as Denmark's leading zinc die-casting foundry. Route visitors to the right section based on their intent. Convert awareness into inquiry.

## Audiences
- **Engineers** evaluating technical capability → route to Services, Why Zinc
- **Procurement managers** comparing suppliers → route to Cases, Contact
- **Decision makers** assessing credibility → show Trust indicators, About

## Required Content Elements

### Primary Value Proposition
- **What:** Single clear statement of what Linimatic does and why they're the right choice
- **Must communicate:** Zinc die-casting + Denmark + precision + heritage (since 1967)
- **CTA:** Primary (Request Quote) and secondary (Explore Services)

### Trust Indicators
- **What:** Key credibility metrics displayed prominently
- **Data points:** Founded 1967, 12M+ parts/year, 12 hot-chamber machines, ISO 9001 certified, full-service capability
- **Purpose:** Instant credibility for first-time visitors

### Services Overview
- **What:** Summary of core service capabilities
- **Content:** 6-10 service categories with brief descriptions
- **Each service needs:** Name, 1-2 sentence description, link to detail page
- **Purpose:** Show breadth of capability; help visitors find relevant service

### Proof of Capability
- **What:** Featured case studies or client outcomes
- **Content:** 2-4 case studies showing different industries/applications
- **Each case needs:** Industry label, project title, one key metric, link to full case
- **Purpose:** Move from claims to evidence

### Material Education Teaser
- **What:** Brief intro to zinc die-casting advantages
- **Content:** 3-4 key advantages of zinc (tolerances, die life, cycle times, surface finish)
- **Link to:** Full Why Zinc page
- **Purpose:** Capture awareness-stage search traffic; educate new visitors

### Company News (Recommended)
- **What:** Latest 2-3 news items or blog posts
- **Purpose:** Signal active, growing company

### Final Conversion Prompt
- **What:** Clear call-to-action with promise (e.g., "Response within 24 hours")
- **CTAs:** Quote form + direct phone contact

## SEO Requirements
- Single H1 containing primary keyword (zinc die-casting + foundry/manufacturer + Denmark)
- Internal links to all major sections
- Organization + LocalBusiness schema
- Image alt text on all visual elements

## Content That Does NOT Belong on Homepage
- Detailed technical specifications (belongs on service pages)
- Full company history (belongs on about page)
- Pricing information
- Long-form educational content (belongs on Why Zinc)
